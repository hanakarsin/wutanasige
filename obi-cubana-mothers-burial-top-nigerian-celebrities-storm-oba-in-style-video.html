Top Nigerian Celebrities Storm Oba In Style |Video

The ongoing burial of popular Nigerian billionaire, Obi Iyiegbu better known as Obi Cubana’s mother which is undoubtedly one of the biggest event in Nigeria this weekend was graced by a good number of top Nigerian celebrities and affluent Nigerians.

Naija News earlier reported that Obi Cubana lost his mother in December 2020, and laid her to rest on July 16th in Oba, Anambra state.

Preparations for the burial caught the attention of many after the club owner received 246 cows from his friends.

Amongst well wishes and friends who graced the ceremony are top Nigerian celebrities who displayed their wealth at the event by spraying and showcasing the wads of cash to be sprayed.

Some celebrities were seen throwing wads of cash to guests who scampered to pick money from the floor, while others entertained.
