Felisha Cooper - Age, Family, Bio

Television and soap opera actress most well known for her portrayal of Sasha in the long running series The Bold and the Beautiful. She is also well recognized for her recurring role in the TNT drama The Last Ship.

Before Fame

She was discovered by her manager only three weeks after she'd moved to Los Angeles. She secured her first major role in the 2013 film All Cheerleaders Die.

Trivia

She has had guest roles on a number of popular series like Big Time Rush The Hustle, and Criminal Minds.

Family Life

Her mother, Kimberly Gibson, was a culinary teacher and her father, Gene Cooper, was an army drill sergeant. She has an older sister named Paris and a younger brother named Bobby.

Associated With

She acted alongside Travis Van Winkle during her stint on the Michael Bay produced series The Last Ship.
