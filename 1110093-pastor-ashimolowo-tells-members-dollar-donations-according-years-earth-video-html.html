Pastor Ashimolowo tells members to make dollar donations according to their years on earth (video)

Some Nigerians are not happy with Pastor Matthew Ashimolowo of Kingsway International Christian Center after a video of him telling his church members to donate money according to their ages, hit the internet.

The pastor, who allegedly worth over $10m is seen telling his church members to donate according to the number of years they have lived on earth.
