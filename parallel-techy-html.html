Techy - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube star and streamer who is best recognized for the family-friendly Fortnite gameplay and montage videos he posts to his Techy YouTube channel. He has accumulated over 1.6 million subscribers on the video platform. His montage videos have featured background music by artists like Xxxtentacion and Lil Nas X. 

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in November 2014.

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube videos is set to the Y2K and Bbno$ song "LALALA," and has received over 3 million views. He has stated that he loves burritos. He has also accumulated over 60,000 followers on Instagram for his Fortnite avatar photos. 

Family Life

His girlfriend has been known online as Strawbys.

Associated With

He has collaborated with fellow Fortnite player Andeh on his YouTube channel.
