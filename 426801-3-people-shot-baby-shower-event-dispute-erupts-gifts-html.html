3 People Shot at Baby Shower Event after Dispute Erupts over Gifts

  • A baby shower in the United States turned tragic after a heated brawl over the baby gifts
  • The family and friends had an altercation that soon turned deadly after a suspect unleashed a gun and shot at the crowd
  • Three people were injured in the incident and the suspect was also arrested

An event that was meant to celebrate a soon-to-be-born baby turned tragic after a suspect shot three people over a brawl that erupted over gifts.

All was going well in the baby shower that was taking place in Pennsylvania in the United States on Saturday, September 18, until when the family bitterly disagreed about the baby gifts.

According to a report by CNN, the altercation soon turned physical and in a blink of an eye, a suspect unleashed a gun and shot into the crowd of about 25 family members and friends who had gathered to celebrate the unborn baby.
