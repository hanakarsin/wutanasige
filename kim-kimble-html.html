Kim Kimble - Age, Family, Bio

Celebrity stylist and entrepreneur who stars on the WE TV reality television series L.A. Hair which follows her in her famous Kimble Hair Studio.

Before Fame

She was a shampoo girl for her mother growing up. Her first celebrity styling job was with Halle Berry while Berry was acting in the film BAPS.

Trivia

Her beauty techniques and innovative styles have been featured in print for titles such as Vanity Fair, Essence, Glamour, Elle, and Vogue.

Family Life

She has two sisters and three brothers. She has a son who is a sports fanatic.

Associated With

Celebrity clients of hers include Beyonce, Lady Gaga, Shakira, Mary J. Blige, and Kerry Washington, among others.
