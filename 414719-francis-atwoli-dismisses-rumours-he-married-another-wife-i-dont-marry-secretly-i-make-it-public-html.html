Francis Atwoli Dismisses Rumours He Married Another Wife: &quot;I Don't Marry Secretly, I Make It Public&quot;

- Central Organization of Trade Unions (COTU) boss Francis Atwoli is madly in love with his younger wife, Mary Kilobi, and nothing can change that

- Last weekend, Mary and Atwoli were in Watamu, Kilifi county, to celebrate his birthday

- The couple enjoyed every moment spent in the coastal city and even went on a yacht cruise with Cabinet Secretary, Ministry of Devolution and ASALs Eugene Wamalwa and one of Atwoli's sons

- Rumours of Atwoli marrying another woman surfaced on Monday and went viral all over social media

Central Organization of Trade Unions (COTU) secretary-general Francis Atwoli has denied rumours that he secretly married a 23-year-old lady over the weekend.

Atwoli and his third wife, Mary Kilobi, were in Watamu, Kilifi county, to celebrate his birthday last weekend.

In a series of photos and videos shared on Atwoli and Mary's social accounts, the couple enjoyed their vacation. They even went on a yacht cruise.

The excited Atwoli took to social media to thank his family and best friend for making his special day memorable.

"May I take this opportunity to say a BIG THANK YOU for all your warm birthday wishes. Sincere gratitude to my wife Mary Atwoli, @EugeneLWamalwa and my son Philipo, who took me on a yacht trip around the Indian Ocean," Atwoli wrote.

Atwoli dismissed the rumours going round, saying there is no problem marrying another woman. He said if has to would, then he would do it publicly.

"That is a lazy rumour from some idle Kenyan. I was at the coast with my wife Mary (Kilobi Atwoli). In any case, there is no problem in marrying but if I have to do that I do it publicly not hiding from anyone," Atwoli exclusively told TUKO.co.ke.
