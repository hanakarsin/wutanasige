Natasha Bure - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube personality who publishes lifestyle, fashion and beauty videos to her self-titled YouTube channel, which has earned over 260,000 subscribers. She has promoted for the Glasses USA website on her channel. 

Before Fame

She is the daughter of actress Candace Cameron-Bure of Full House fame. They attended the Teen Choice Awards together in August of 2014.

Trivia

She appeared in an episode of Make It or Break It in 2011. She auditioned to be on The Voice season 11 in 2016. She has also been popular on her self-titled Instagram account, which has featured outfit and lifestyle photos for her over 550,000 followers. 

Family Life

Her father is former NHL player Valeri Bure and she has younger siblings named Lev and Maksim.

Associated With

She is the niece of Kirk Cameron, Bridgette Cameron, Melissa Cameron and Pavel Bure.
