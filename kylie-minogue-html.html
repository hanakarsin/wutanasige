Kylie Minogue - Age, Family, Bio

Grammy Award-winning pop star who released the biggest Australian hit of the decade, "The Loco-Motion." She had a comeback in the 2000s with the #1 single "Can't Get You Out of My Head." She has also appeared in such films as San Andreas, Holy Motors and Moulin Rouge. 

Before Fame

She acted in several Australian soap operas as a child and gained recognition from her role on Neighbours. 

Trivia

She won a Mo Award for Australian Entertainer of the Year. As fashion icon, she partnered with H&M for a line of beachwear, and then launched her own lingerie brand called Love Kylie.
