Trevor Noah ranked fourth highest paid comedian in the world by Forbes

- Popular American comedian, Trevor Noah, emerged the fourth highest earning personality in the industry with KSh 2.8 billion

- The revenue was calculated between Friday, June 1, 2018 and Saturday, June 1, 2019

- Kevin Hart, Jim Gaffigan and Sebastian Maniscalco were among the top 10 highest earning comedians

Trevor Noah, a popular South African stand-up comedian, has made it into the list of top 10 high earners in the industry in the period 2018-2019.

Trevor emerged fourth in the list with a revenue of KSh 2.8 billion.

According to Forbes, the income calculation was done between Friday, June 1, 2018 and Saturday, June 1, 2019.

"Despite the demands of hosting a late-night show, Noah made more than 70 stops across the world and had his second Netflix special last fall', Forbes wrote.

On the list of top 10 highest earners were popular comedians such as Kevin Hart who took first place, Jim Gaffigan, Sebastian Maniscalco among others.

Comedian and actress Amy Schumer was the only woman who made it to the list.

TUKO.co.ke learnt most of the US-based South African comedian's earnings came from his business ventures including his Netflix show, Son of Patricia, his stand-up tour, TV hosting job and his book, Born a Crime 2016.

It is worth noting that the figures are pretax, which means fees for agents, managers and lawyers were not deducted.

The earnings estimates are based on data from Pollstar Pro as well as interviews with industry insiders, according to Forbes.

Below is the full list:

1. Kevin Hart – $59 million

2. Jerry Seinfeld – $41 million

3. Jim Gaffigan – $30 million

4. Trevor Noah – $28 million

5. Sebastian Maniscalco – $26 million

6. Gabriel Iglesias – $22 million

7. Amy Schumer – $21 million

8. Terry Fator – $17 million

9. Jeff Dunham – $15 million

10. Aziz Ansari – $13 million
