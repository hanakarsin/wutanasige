MP Moha Jicho Pevu gives police officers goats, KSh 100K as early Christmas gift

Nyali MP Mohammed Ali alias Moha Jicho Pevu has kicked off the Christmas mood with gifts for police officers in his constituency.

The outspoken politician took to his social media where he shared photos gifting the officers with the animals as well as KSh 100,000 in appreciation for their dedication to service delivery.

While acknowledging police work was not an easy job, the lawmaker noted he was appreciating their work with gifts.

"Police work is a dangerous, challenging and often thankless job. Every day they are thrust into difficult situations and expected to make decisions in seconds. Met officers from my constituency, gave out early Christmas gifts and my personal donation of KSh 100,000. Happy holidays," he said.

Apart from the money and goats, other items that were given to the officers included bottles of soda and rice.

However, despite his goodwill, some Kenyans were quick to point out there was no need to show off or to gift the officers in full glare of cameras.

Others congratulated the MP for the good gesture of supporting the men in uniform.
