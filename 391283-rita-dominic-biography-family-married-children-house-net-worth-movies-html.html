Rita Dominic biography: Family, married, children, house, net worth, movies

Rita Dominic is one of the most familiar names and faces in Nollywood. She is a super-talented Nigerian actress and movie producer. She also doubles up as the goodwill ambassador for Intra African Trade Fair (IATF) and the co-founder of The Audrey Silva Company (TASC).

Rita began acting from a young age. She made numerous appearances in school plays and children's TV shows in Imo State. She has grown into one of the leading actresses in the country with more than 200 movies to her credit.

The actress was born on 12th July 1975 in Mbaise, Imo State, in the southeastern region of Nigeria. She is a member of the royal family of Waturuocha of Aboh Mbaise local government.

Rita Dominic's parents (late) were medical practitioners. Her father was a medical doctor while her mother was a nursing officer. She is the youngest of her four siblings whose names and whereabouts aren't publicly known.

Rita joined Ikot Ekpene Primary School in Akwa Ibom State where she attained her first school leaving certificate. She enrolled at the Federal Government College where she obtained her West African Senior School Certificate.

The star later joined the University of Port Harcourt in Rivers State Nigeria. She graduated in 1999 with a Bachelor's Degree in Theatre Arts.

Career

Rita started performing on stage while still a little girl. She appeared in school plays, drama and kids TV shows in her state of origin. She joined the Nigerian movie industry, commonly referred to as Nollywood in 1998.
