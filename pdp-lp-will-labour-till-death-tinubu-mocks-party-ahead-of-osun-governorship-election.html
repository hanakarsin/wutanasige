PDP, LP Will Labour Till Death

The Presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress, Bola Tinubu, on Tuesday, threw shades at the Peoples Democratic Party and the Labour Party ahead of the governorship election in Osun State.

Tinubu on Tuesday said the main opposition party and LP would labour till they die.

The former Governor of Lagos State stated this while campaigning for the governorship candidate of the APC, Gboyega Oyetola ahead of the June 16th election.

Oyetola would be contesting against the candidate of the PDP, Ademola Adeleke, Lasun Yusuf of LP and other governorship candidates.

Tinubu during the campaign called on the people of Osun State to vote for the APC, the same way the people of Ekiti supported the party during its governorship election.

He called on the people of Osun State to think of their children when voting and not mind the PDP and other mushroom parties.

Tinubu said, “I ask you to please do the same thing you did in Ekiti. Come out with your PVCs and vote massively for APC.

“The voting is now in your hand. Be very vigilant. Be watchful. You will not do it in vain.

“Think about your children and vote accordingly so you can see the future. Come out en masse. Don’t mind PDP and other mushroom parties, parties like Labour; they will labour till they die. God will not make you labourers.”
