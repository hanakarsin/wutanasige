R5 - Members, Ages, Trivia

Four brothers, their sister, and a family friend that came together to play music for a living. They were named the Next Big Thing at The Next Big Thing Awards in 2013. 

Trivia

Their first headlining tour was the 2012 West Coast Tour. They covered The Script's "Breakeven" on their Dancing Out My Pants Tour in 2013.
