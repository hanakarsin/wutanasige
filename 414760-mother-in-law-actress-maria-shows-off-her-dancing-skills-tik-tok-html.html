Mother-In-Law Actress Maria Shows Off Her Dancing Skills on Tik Tok

- Mother-In-Law actress Maria posted a video dancing to Mejja's hit Siskii

- Holding a wooden cooking stick, the actress and a colleague slowly moved to the beat

- The video, which Mejja reposted, elicited positive reactions from netizens

Mejja’s hit song Siskii has taken the country by storm, courtesy of the relatable lyrics describing life in a relationship.

Unsurprisingly, the song has caught on among TikTok users and even celebrities.

Popular Mother In Law actress Maria posted a video dancing along to the song by Mejja.

In a TikTok video, the actress, who plays a house help in Mother In Law, moved to the beat with a colleague.

"Unachoma picha kwa jirani (you are embarrassing yourself in front of the neighbours)," the colleague rapped along to Mejja. "I don't care," Maria responded, completing the verse.

The actress put her dancing skills on full display as she slowly moved to the catchy beat.

“New vibes!” Maria captioned the video.

Charting own course

After The Kansoul broke up, Mejja launched his YouTube channel four months ago, and it is doing quite well.

The first song he uploaded was Ulimi Yangu which currently has 1.4 million views. Siskii, released on February 19, has over 4 million views.

His latest hit, dubbed Tabia Za Wakenya, dropped on May 18 and has over 2 million views.

Challenges

While his music has been well received, Mejja has not had it easy. In May, YouTube pulled down one of his songs dubbed Naitwa Mejja featuring Nasha Travis.

Responding to inquiries from fans, Mejja said that they had sampled a beat, and the owner complained to YouTube.

The video platform pulled down the song, and according to Mejja, the sample owner refused to negotiate to have the video back up.

A week ago, an upcoming artist accused Mejja of stealing the Tabia Za Wakenya song from him.

Revina claimed the rapper had colluded with music producer Vicky Pondis to steal the song.
