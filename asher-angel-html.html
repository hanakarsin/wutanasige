Asher Angel - Age, Family, Bio

Disney Channel personality who landed the starring role of Jonah Beck on Andi Mack. He'd go on to star as Billy Batson in the remake of Shazam! in 2019.

Before Fame

He has been active in theater, performing in more than 10 productions put on by the Desert Stages Theatre. 

Trivia

He was a guest on the shows Criminal Minds: Beyond Borders and Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn. He is extremely popular on TikTok, with over 7 million fans on his asherangel account. He is also a singer and released his debut single "One Thought Away" with Wiz Khalifa in June 2019.

Family Life

He has younger siblings named Avi and London. His parents are Jody and Coco Angel. He was in a relationship with Jules LeBlanc from 2019 to May 2020. In December 2020, he began a relationship with Caroline Gregory. 

Associated With

He was cast alongside Sofia Wylie in Disney's Andi Mack.
