Rashid Abdalla Emotionally Remembers Late Dad with Sweet Tbt Photo: &quot;I Miss You&quot;

  • It is no doubt that Rashid Abdalla had a strong attachment to his dad in his boyhood before the old man passed away
  • The media personality lost his father way back in May 1996 and he still finds it tormenting up to today
  • Posting on his Instagram stories, Abdalla shared a photo of his late dad to express how much he misses him

Citizen TV Swahili news anchor, Rashid Abdalla, has taken to social media to cherish his late father who passed away more than two decades ago.

Remembering the old man, the celebrated journalist shared a cute throwback photo of his deceased dad and wrote in the photo that he misses him dearly.

It is not easy

In his previous post seen by TUKO.co.ke, Abdalla revealed his father died on May 5, 1996. According to the heartfelt post, it was not easy for them after his loss but had to accept God's will.

"A date like today in the year 1996 my died passed away. It was not easy and still is not easy but God's work has no faults and whatever he plans no one can destroy," wrote Abdalla on Instagram.

He pleaded with his fans to intervene for God's forgiveness on his dad's soul.

"May you find time to make a good prayer for God to forgive him in his death. May Allah protect him from the punishment of the grave and the punishment of fire. May the Lord make his tomb a better house than the rest of the house and Inshallah on the day of reckoning, he and I be given our book in the right hand," added Abdalla.

Abdalla raves about wife Lulu

As previously reported by TUKO.co.ke, the news anchor was excited after spotting his wife, Lulu Hassan looking glamorous on the red carpet.

This was after the mother of three who is also his co-anchor stepped out looking like a goddess in a stunning dress during the launch of a new phone.

Sharing his wife's snap, Abdalla happily referred to his wife as; "Mrs Rashid Abdalla", reminding everyone that the beautiful lass is his wife.
