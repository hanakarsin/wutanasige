'He Was Poisoned' - May Edochie's Relative Alleges Cause Of Kambili's Death (Video)

A relative of May Edochie, first wife of Nollywood actor, Yul Edochie, has revealed what led to the unfortunate death of her 16-year-old son, Kambi.

It would be recalled that May’s son died on March 30 after he developed a seizure in school while playing football with his schoolmates.

The mother of three via her Instagram on Sunday paid tribute to her son.

In a video, a relative of May, during an Instagram live call, disclosed that Kambili was allegedly poisoned with a burger and drink delivered to him at his school.

According  to the lady, after eating the meal, he reportedly complained to his friend about how he was feeling.

She added that the boy didn’t die of seizure as those behind his death threatened late Kambi’s friend family, who was with him when he received the package of food.

Recall, May recently moved to dissolve her 18-year marriage with the actor.

The now mother of three and her lawyer reportedly visited a court on Monday, July 11th, to submit a petition against Yul.

The viral reports also alleged that Yul refused the petition, which had caused a delay in the filing of the proceedings.

Adding to it, Instagram blogger, Gistlover alleged that the court granted full custody of their three kids to May Edochie and also granted her full custody of their mansion.

The court reportedly warned the actor never to go near the mansion to disturb his first wife.
