Danielle Deadwyler - Age, Family, Bio

American actress most famous for her role as LaQuita Maxwell on the OWN series The Haves and the Have Nots. She is also a respected multimedia artist whose works have been featured in venues like the Mint Gallery, Whitespace Gallery and The Luminary.

Before Fame

She grew up in Atlanta, Georgia and earned a Master's of Arts in American Studies from Columbia University.

Trivia

In 2013, she performed the one-woman live show (dis)possessed.

Family Life

She has been married and has a son.

Associated With

She co-stars with Tika Sumpter on The Haves and the Have Nots.
