Saraki Speaks On Whom He Prefers Between Wizkid And Davido

Former Senate President of Nigeria, Dr. Abubakar Bukola Saraki has smartly refused to be drawn into the debate on whom he prefers between two of Nigeria’s top musicians, Davido and Wizkid.

Saraki had taken to Twitter on Friday to disclose that he is enjoying one of the tracks in the newly released ‘Made In Lagos’ album by Wizkid.

He wrote: “Wizkid’s #MadeInLagos is a brilliant end to what has been a long and eventful month for all of us. Currently vibing to my favorite track on the album ‘#Grace.’”

Wizkid’s #MadeInLagos is a brilliant end to what has been a long and eventful month for all of us. Currently vibing to my favorite track on the album ‘#Grace.’ pic.twitter.com/BfIIsIVPj8

— Dr. Abubakar Bukola Saraki (@bukolasaraki) October 30, 2020

However, someone in the comment section attempted to drag him out by asking him whom he prefers between Davido and Wizkid.

Saraki in his response admonished that rather than be dragged into comparison and preferences, both singers have been making Nigeria proud and should be supported by everyone to continue to represent the country positively on the international stage.

Saraki replied: “Over the years, @Davido has been like a son to me. He, Wizkid and other young Nigerian artistes keep pushing the boundaries in our entertainment sector and making all of us proud! I also can’t wait for David’s album to drop next month!”
