Nigeria Ahead Of Ghana In FIFA Ranking (Full List)

The male national team of Nigeria, Super Eagles are ahead of the Black Stars of Ghana in the latest FIFA world rankings despite missing out on the 2022 Qatar World Cup.

The Super Eagles ranked 30th in March ranking released by FIFA on Thursday.

The Black Stars of Ghana ranks 60th best team in the world, while Senegal national team rank number one in Africa and 20th best team in the world.

Morocco ranks the second best team in the continent, while Nigeria is the third-best team in Africa.

Egypt, Tunisia, and Cameroon are in fourth, fifth, and sixth place, respectively.

Brazil has been ranked as the best team in the world, followed by Belgium, France, Argentina, England, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Mexico, Netherlands, making the top 10.

The likes of Denmark, Germany, Uruguay, Switzerland, USA, Croatia, Colombia, Wales, Sweden, and Senegal rank from 11-20 on the table.
