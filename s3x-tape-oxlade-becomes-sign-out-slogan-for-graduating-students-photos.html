Oxlade Becomes 'Sign Out Slogan' For Graduating Students (Photos)

Some unidentified students of the University of Ilorin (Unilorin) have stirred reaction online after they used popular singer, Oxlade, who was involved in a leaked s3x tape scandal, as a slogan for their signing out on Thursday.

Oxlade in the past few days have been a major point of discussion after he was found in a compromising moment with a lady.

While some praised the singer for the incident, others challenged him to apologize for his action.

A few days after, some graduating students have compared their struggle in the university with the sexual potency of the songwriter.

The graduating students on their signing out cloth wrote, “Just like Oxlade, I laid this degree”
