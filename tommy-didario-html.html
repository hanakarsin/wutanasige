Tommy DiDario - Age, Family, Bio

TV show host and lifestyle expert who covers pop news, trends and fashion. He gained notoriety as an on-air contributor for Rachael Ray and The Rachael Ray Show. He has also worked with GW and Sunwarrior and he became the host of Amazon Live. 

Before Fame

He grew up doing theater and has goals of working as both a writer and actor.

Trivia

He is a pescatarian and is an advocate for health and wellness and the LGBTQ community. 

Family Life

His husband is fellow tv personality Gio Benitez.

Associated With

He is a big fan of Hollywood writers and filmmakers such as Judd Apatow, Paul Feig, and more.
