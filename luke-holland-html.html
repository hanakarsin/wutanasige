Luke Holland - Age, Family, Bio

Member of the band The Word Alive who also posts videos to his YouTube channel LukeHollandDrums.

Before Fame

He started playing the drums at the young age of 11 and started posting videos to YouTube in 2009.

Trivia

He endorsed DW Drums, Meinl Cymbals, Vater Percussion & Evans Drumheads.

Family Life

He was born in Peoria, Arizona alongside his brother, Paul, and his sister.

Associated With
