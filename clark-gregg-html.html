Clark Gregg - Age, Family, Bio

Plays Agent Phil Coulson in the Marvel Comics films and in the ABC series Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. He also starred as Richard Campbell on the CBS series The New Adventures of Old Christine.

Before Fame

His first TV appearance came in the 1988 TV movie Lip Service. He appeared in an episode of Law & Order in 1991.

Trivia

He wrote and directed the 2008 film Choke, based on a novel of the same name by Chuck Palahniuk.

Family Life

He married actress Jennifer Grey in 2001. He and Jennifer have a daughter named Stella.

Associated With

He appeared alongside Scarlett Johansson in the 2012 film The Avengers.
