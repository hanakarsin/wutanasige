HealthNut Nutrition - Age, Family, Bio

Lifestyle vlogger who is widely known for her HealthNut Nutrition YouTube channel. She has gained popularity there for her healthy dieting tutorials for entress, appetizers, and desserts alike.

Before Fame

She began her YouTube channel on February 21, 2012.

Trivia

She has amassed more than 1 million subscribers to her YouTube channel. One of her most popular videos there, "What I Eat In a Day at Work | EASY & Healthy Meals," has earned more than 750,000 views.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Canada.

Associated With

She and Alyssia Sheikh are both known for their healthy dieting tutorials and vlogs posted to YouTube.
