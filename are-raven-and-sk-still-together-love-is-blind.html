Are Raven and SK From 'Love Is Blind' Still Together? The Drama Continues (SPOILER)

Are Raven and SK from ‘Love Is Blind’ still together? Season 3 of ‘Love Is Blind’ kicked off with five engaged couples — did this one stick it out?

Spoiler alert: This article contains spoilers for Season 3 of Love Is Blind: After the Altar.

In Season 3 of Love Is Blind, sparks turned into fireworks — and then mostly fell down and drowned, like actual fireworks. Yes, five of the Netflix couples were engaged by Episode 3 after their time in the pods connecting through conversations, but it’s not likely that all of these couples will last in the long run. One of the couples many were hesitant about was Raven and SK.

Bartise openly shared his attraction for Raven, even telling his fiancée, Nancy, about how attracted he is to the pilates instructor. But once he tried to get emotionally close with Raven in the pool, the blond bombshell shut him down like a true queen.

Are Raven and SK from 'Love Is Blind' still together?

In Season 3 of Love Is Blind: After the Altar, Raven and SK were in a long-distance relationship. SK even proposed to her (and she accepted) in Episode 3, which was filmed in fall 2022.

However, a few months later, Raven offered an update: SK and Raven are not still together today. “Since then, a lot has changed. My world is completely different because, you know, SK cheated on me and now our relationship is over,” Raven later told the camera.

She added: “It’s so crazy to watch it back and, like, remember my feelings then. ... I was so happy. I really was so invested in the person that everyone saw and everyone loved, like, that’s the person that I saw and I loved every day too. So, to see everything that has happened since then is so hard because that was not the person that I thought that I was with.”

SK turned Raven down at their wedding on 'Love Is Blind' — but were together at the reunion.

In the reunion episode, both Raven and SK confirmed that they were still an item. "Are you guys dating?" Vanessa Lachey asked both Raven and SK.

"Yes," the pair shared while smiling.

They went on to share that they watched Season 3 together.

The news didn't come as a surprise to fans of the couple. Many social media sleuths had been analyzing Raven's TikTok activity. Since Raven has a fun personality and has used Love Is Blind for content, she made a ton of videos ranging from throwing funny shade at SK for turning her down at the altar to trolling people calling her out for her expectations in a relationship.

Fans also noticed that Raven spoke kindly about SK on social media and had even shared how much she loved his mother.

But alas, it simply wasn't meant to be.
