Michael Strahan Isn't Just a TV Personality He's a Doting Father of Four

In addition to being a former athlete and current TV personality, Michael Strahan is the proud father of four kids. Meet all of his children here!

You may know Michael Strahan as a former New York Giants football star or a host on shows like Good Morning America and The $100,000 Pyramid. But when the multhyphenate isn't busy with his bustling career, he's a proud dad to four children!

Michael shares four kids with his two ex-wives. Meet his entire brood below!

The eldest of Michael Strahan's children is Tanita Strahan.

Tanita is both Michael's oldest child and eldest daughter. Michael welcomed Tanita with his first ex-wife, Wanda Hutchins, to whom he was married from 1992 to 1996. Tanita was born the year before her famous dad was drafted to the New York Giants.

According to her Instagram account, she's an illustrator who resides in LA. You can check out a lot of her beautiful art on her page.

Michael Strahan Jr. is his only son.

Michael Strahan Jr., who was born on Sept. 12, 1994, is Michael's only son and his second eldest child. His mother is Michael's first ex-wife, Wanda, so Tanita is his full sister.

According to his Instagram account, he is a proud UTSA (University of Texas at San Antonio) alumnus. He's also appeared on GMA as a guest alongside his father in the past!

Isabella Strahan is one of Michael Strahan's twin daughters.

Isabella is one of Michael's twin daughters from his second marriage to Jean Muggli. Isabella and her sister, Sophia, were born on Oct. 28, 2004. They are Tanita and Michael Jr.'s half-sisters.

In 2022, Isabella made her runway modeling debut for Sherri Hill. She planned to go to USC for college, but in January 2024, she bravely revealed on GMA alongside her father that she had been diagnosed with the cancerous brain tumor medulloblastoma the previous October. She said that amid her ongoing treatment, she hopes to "be a voice" for those who are going through radiation and chemotherapy.

Sophia Strahan is Isabella's twin sister.

Sophia is Isabella's twin sister, who was also born in 2004 to Michael and Jean. She and Isabella are Michael's youngest children.

According to her Instagram account, she's based in NYC but goes to school at Duke, and it appears that loves traveling all parts of the world.

When Michael isn't spending time with his children, he's hanging out with Enzo and Zuma, the adorable family pups! "Nothing better than Enzo and Zuma to put a smile on my face…. Of course they wanted a treat so it wasn’t unconditional. LOL," he wrote alongside a sweet pic of him and the dogs around the holidays.
