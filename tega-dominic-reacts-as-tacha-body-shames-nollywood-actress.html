Tega Dominic Reacts As Tacha Body-shames Nollywood Actress

Former Big Brother Naija Housemate, Tega Dominic has reacted to a statement from her colleague, Tacha body-shaming a Nollywood actress.

Naija News recalls that Tacha had come down hard on Monalisa Stephen over an offensive statement.

Monalisa, in a recent interview with Pulse, said Tacha would have been a nobody without the BBNaija reality TV show.

The movie star stated that Tacha would probably be twerking on the internet for cheap money if not for the BBNaija platform.

She said, “Tacha is a very confident woman, she knows what she is doing.

“Something made you. Without BBN, she would still be there twerking her ass for some change. They made her, so it’s okay for you to respect that. BBN made Tacha”

Displeased with the thespian statement, Tacha took to her Instagram account and wrote, “If you run as much as you run you mouth you would have been in better shape‼ Why didn’t they make Ella?? If you don’t stfu

“How many times do I have to say HOW GRATEFUL I AM FOR THE PLATFORM‼ Oh no, I’m suppose to resume at their office and clean desks and tables daily‼ You’ll forever be dum*b‼ iPUT IN WORK.”

Sharing her thoughts on her colleague’s outburst, Tega argued that it is impossible to spit on someone face and expect a solemn reaction.

Speaking via the comment section of a popular blog where the story was shared, she wrote, “Everyone talking about her reaction to action, how about the action. You can’t spit on her face and expect a solemn reaction.”
