Nia Sioux - Age, Family, Bio

Child dancer who gained reality fame as a regular on Dance Moms. She released her first single "Star In Your Own Life" in 2015. She stars as Emma Barber on the TV series The Bold and the Beautiful and as Sonitas in the film The Code. 

Before Fame

She made her Dance Moms debut at the age of 10. 

Trivia

She earned a scholarship for being the most improved student in 2012. 

Family Life

She is the daughter of Evan and Holly Frazier and she has two siblings, Evan Jr. and William. She dated social star Bryce Xavier up until mid 2018.  

Associated With

She became friends with fellow Dance Moms star Madison Ziegler.
