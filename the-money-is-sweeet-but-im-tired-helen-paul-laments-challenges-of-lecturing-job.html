'The Money Is Sweeet But I'm Tired'

Nigerian comedienne cum actress, Helen Paul, has opened up about the challenges of her new lecturing job in the United States of America.

Naija News recalls that in June 2022, the stand-up comedienne announced she has been appointed as a lecturer at the Heart International Bible University, US.

However, in a video shared on her Instagram page on Wednesday, Helen questioned who advised her to change her career, stressing that the entertainment industry is fun and interesting.

According to her, all she does is gaze at students’ books and hardly has time to take care of herself or go out.

Helen Paul added that irrespective of the boredom, she is happy about the money she is currently making.

Tekno Contemplates Quitting Smoking

Meanwhile, popular Nigerian singer, Augustine Miles Kelechi with the stage name Tekno has hinted at going on a ‘no weed challenge’ for 30 days.

The singer shared his decision on his Insta Stories on Wednesday, October 5.

He revealed that he is planning to stay away from marijuana for 30 days after which he will decide to continue or stop.

He stated that the first 24 hours has being quite difficult as he found it hard to sleep.

He said, “My first 24 hours of not smoking cannabis, marijuana, weed or whatever you decide to call it. It was a little hard to sleep the first night but this is 24 hours. I am about to go another 24 hours; I am trying to go 30 days not smoking weed.

At the end of these 30 days, I will decide if I want to keep smoking or if I don’t want to smoke anymore but I will come back here to keep you guys updated every day.”
