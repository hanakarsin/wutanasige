Tara Fares - Trivia, Family, Bio

Model and fashion aficionado whose frequent selfies have earned her followers on her its.tarafares Instagram account. She was voted Baghdad's beauty queen in 2015. 

Before Fame

She first began posting selfies and fashion photos to Instagram in May 2016.  

Trivia

In addition to Instagram, she is known on Snapchat and Twitter by the handle Tarafaresss. 

Family Life

She has lived in Dubai. She was fatally shot at age 22.

Associated With

Both she and Fahima Karmostaji are popular Instagrammers from the U.A.E.
