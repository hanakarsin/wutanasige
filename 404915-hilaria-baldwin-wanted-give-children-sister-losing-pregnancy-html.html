Hilaria, through social media, disclosed for the first time why they decided to expand their family even after her miscarriage.

In an Instagram post seen by TUKO.co.ke, the yoga guru revealed that her pregnancy loss was the major reason why she and her hubby Alec decided to have yet another kid.

Hilaria, on Thursday, March 4, said that her other babies were very devastated when she lost her pregnancy at four months in 2019.

TUKO.co.ke understands Hilaria recently welcomed the couple’s sixth child through a surrogate, barely six months after giving birth to her son Eduardo.

“When I found out our baby had died, I told our children that their sister was going to come, just not at that time. Nothing will ever replace her, but two wonderful souls have come into our lives, and we are humbled to know them.” Hilaria disclosed.

''They craved so much to have a little sister and held out hope for another sibling, and wound up getting two,'' she added.

She also used the opportunity to pamper her children for being very brave when she lost her pregnancy.

“Our children were brave during our time of tremendous grief—braver than us…and they held out hope. To experience and accept life’s ups and downs is challenging, but a reality that all of us have no choice but to receive and process. I have learned that often our children are wiser than we are and their wisdom has guided us.” Hilaria stated.

Her statement came barely days after she welcomed her sixth born child.

TUKO.co.ke earlier reported how Hilaria shared the good news on Instagram with a photo of her and the kids together next to the newborn with smiles on their cute faces.
