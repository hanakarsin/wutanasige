I met my wife on the dance floor, but it wasnt easy - Governor Kiraitu Murungi

- Murungi is married to Priscilla with whom they have four children

- He said he danced better than the man in the company of his would-be wife and won her heart

- The seasoned politician enjoy sleeping as his hobby and listening to Kikuyu music

Meru Governor Kiraitu Murungi has revealed his unmatched classic dance moves during his heydays won him a woman who later became the mother to his children.

Speaking at the weekly Churchil comedy show on Sunday, April 22, Kiraitu amused viewers with his revelations he was a better dancer than the man in the company of his would-be wife.

“I met my wife on the dance floor, but it wasn’t easy because she had come with another man. I think I out-danced him and the rest is history,” he said.

The seasoned politician enjoy sleeping as his hobby and listening to Kikuyu music specifically singer Joseph Kamaru's songs.

“My hobbies include sleeping. I love resting. I also like it when my company is a happy one. Like you and these people," he added.

On matters development, Kiraitu urged youth from Meru county to take advantage of the vast opportunities in agribusiness through the Meru Youth Services.

He expressed optimism the service will promote self-reliance and become the agent of change in the Miraa rich region.

Kiraitu joined politics in 1992 on a FORD Kenya party ticket after the then opposition leader, Jaramogi Oginga Odinga prevailed upon him.
