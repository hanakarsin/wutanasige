Alex Warren (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Comedic video content creator most well known for self-titled YouTube channel where he posts vlogs as well as stunt and skateboarding videos. He is also popular on TikTok and Instagram and he is known for using the hashtag #WarHeads in his posts. 

Before Fame

He grew up skateboarding and would first share his content to Instagram as early as May of 2015. 

Trivia

He was previously a member of the collaborative TikTok group The Hype House. He has over 15 million TikTok fans and has earned over 2 million followers on his alexwaarren Instagram account. He released his debut original song "One More I Love You" in June 2021.

Family Life

He is from Carlsbad, California and has siblings named Ashley and Grant. He lost his dad to cancer when he was 9 years old. He began a relationship with Instagrammer Kouvr Annon in 2018.

Associated With

He is a friend of model and skater Joshua Forsberg.
