Alice Young - Age, Family, Bio

Wife of Neil deGrasse Tyson, who works as a Mathematical Physicist. She began dating Neil while they were graduate students. 

Before Fame

She got her undergraduate degree from Rice University and received her graduate degree from the University of Texas. 

Trivia

She and Neil got married in 1998. She worked as an IT manager for Bloomberg LLC in New York. She stood by her husband's side as he faced allegations of misconduct in 2018. 

Family Life

She and Neil have a daughter named Miranda, who was named after one of the moons of Uranus, as well as a son named Travis. 

Associated With

Her husband has appeared on HBO's Real Time with Bill Maher.
