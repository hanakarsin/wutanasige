Akothee Happily Discloses All Her Daughters Now Have Houses: &quot;Let Me Enjoy Life&quot;

- Akothee is a happy mum after all her daughters ventured out of her house

- The mother of five shared a throwback photo taken eight years ago when they were still struggling

- She patted herself on the back for a job well done, adding that it is time she enjoyed life

Parenting is not easy, and hacking it is proof of one’s mettle.

Philanthropic singer Akothee is a happy mother after all her daughters moved out of her house.

The Hayakuhusu singer shared a throwback photo from eight years ago alongside her girls, saying back then, she wished someone would help her pay the bills.

“During this time, I wish someone volunteered to even buy me your shoes or send me your pocket money,” she wrote to her daughters.

According to the mother of five, the greatest thing she has achieved is constantly being in her children’s life. Akothee said it would not have been possible without God’s grace.

She expressed pride in seeing them grow into women, saying it is time for her to enjoy life.

“The thing I have achieved all my life with God's help is to be in the life of my children throughout their childhood. Seeing them grow into women who can make their own decisions has made me proud.“All of them have their houses now. School fee paid and let me enjoy my life,” she wrote.

Fighting fire with fire

Akothee is a straight shooter and lived up to the reputation on April 28 when one of her followers allegedly insulted her.

The philanthropist made it clear she would not be messed with before urging the woman not to block her.

“Lea, thank you for the insults. I swear to never insult you even if I was paid to. But I would like you to tell me, how are you going to defend yourself?Today you will know that if stupidity was a person, then it is me, Madam Boss,” Akothee wrote in a since-deleted post.
