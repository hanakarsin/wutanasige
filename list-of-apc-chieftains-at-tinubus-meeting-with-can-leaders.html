List Of APC Chieftains At Tinubus Meeting With CAN Leaders

The Presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu, on Wednesday, met with the leadership of the Christian Association of Nigeria (CAN).

Recall that the Christian body had opposed the Muslim-Muslim Presidential ticket of the ruling party, following Tinubuâ€™s choice of the former Governor of Borno State, Kashim Shettima, as his running mate.

Naija News earlier reported that CAN President, His Eminence Daniel Okoh, had said the Christian body will be meeting with Tinubu, Atiku Abubakar of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), and Peter Obi of the Labour Party (LP).

The CAN President had said the presidential dialogue is part of engagements for the forthcoming 2023 general election and to ensure that the APC standard bearer clearly understands the concerns of Nigerian Christians with a view to proposing policies and programmes to address them.

Tinubu arrived the CAN headquarters at the Christian Ecumenical Centre in Abuja at about 2.30 pm today to meet with the leaders of the Christian body.

Below is the list of APC chieftains at the meeting.

The wife of the APC Presidential candidate, Oluremi Tinubu, the Speaker of the House of Representatives, Femi Gbajabiamila; Governors Dave Umahi (Ebonyi), Hope Uzodimma (Imo), AbdulRahman AbdulRazaq (Kwara), Abdullahi Ganduje (Kano); the Minister of Special Duties, Senator George Akume; Senate Chief Whip, Orji Kalu, amongst others.
