Christina Hobbs - Trivia, Family, Bio

Novelist known as one half of the writing duo Christina Lauren. She and her friend Lauren Billings have co-authored young adult and adult romance novels. Their titles include Dating You Hating You, Dirty Rowdy Thing, Twice in a Blue Moon, Roomies and the Beautiful series.

Before Fame

She and Lauren Billings met in 2009 while writing fan fiction online. In 2010, they became coauthors. In 2011, they signed with an agent together.

Trivia

She worked as a junior high school counselor.  Her novel The Unhoneymooners spent 14 weeks on the New York Times Bestseller List. In 2021, Deadline announced it had been optioned for a film.

Family Life

She and her husband have a daughter.

Associated With

She and Lauren Layne are both famous romance novelists.
