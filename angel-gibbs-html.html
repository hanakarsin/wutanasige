Angel Gibbs - Age, Family, Bio

Member of the dance crew 8 Flavahz who finished runner-up on the 7th season of America's Best Dance Crew. She was a featured dancer on Dancing with the Stars, The Voice, and America's Got Talent and toured with The World of Dance in 2014 as well.

Before Fame

She started dancing when she was two years old and began working in the industry when she was five. From 2007 until 2009, she was a dancer for Macy's Passport Glamorama.

Trivia

She was featured as a dancer in music videos for artists including Bob Sinclar, Shakira, and Willow Smith, among others.

Family Life

She was born in Anaheim, California. She has sisters named Aisha and Eshe Gibbs.

Associated With

She danced in the iPod commercial featuring Mary J. Blige and her song "Work That."
