Chyna - Trivia, Family, Bio

Born Joan Marie Laurer, she is a former WWE Diva who was a two-time Intercontinental Champion during her career.

Before Fame

She earned a degree in Spanish Literature from the University of Tampa.

Trivia

She wrestled for the New Japan Pro Wrestling promotion and Total N Action Wrestling after leaving the WWE.

Family Life

She had relationships with wrestlers Sean Waltman and Triple H; she grew up with two older siblings, Sonny and Janet.

Associated With

She became the on-screen girlfriend of Eddie Guerrero; Guerrero called her his "Mamacita."
