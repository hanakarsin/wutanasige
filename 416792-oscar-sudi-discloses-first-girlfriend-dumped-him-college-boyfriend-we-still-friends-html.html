Oscar Sudi Discloses First Girlfriend Dumped Him for College Boyfriend: &quot;We're Still Friends&quot;

  • Kapseret MP Oscar Sudi said his first girlfriend left him for another boy with whom she was studying in campus
  • Sudi was then working as a tout in his native area, now Uasin Gishu county
  • Before becoming a tout Sudi had worked as a herd's boy for a councillor in Kesses

Kapseret Member of Parliament Oscar Sudi says he has had lows as it would be with any struggling man.

The vocal MP recalled how he began his life, then working as a tout.

Speaking on NTV's Wicked Edition, Sudi said his situation could not make him counted as those accomplished in life.

He said his first girlfriend left him due to his job, which was then looked down on.

"My salary was KSh 800 per month. I didn't have a girl to date but found her later. But when she went to college, she changed her mind," Sudi said.

Despite losing his girlfriend, Sudi said he remained disciplined until he met his wife, who he married while aged 20 years.

His humble beginnings notwithstanding, Sudi has risen to be one of the known Kenyan politicians.

As previously reported by TUKO.co.ke, the lawmaker was not popular until he gave his shot in the 2013 General Election as an aspirant for Kapseret parliamentary seat.

Despite his tongue lashing habits and all the controversies surrounding him, Sudi has always said his constituents see him as a treasure.

About his riser to affluence, Sudi has always reiterated that he was self-made, attributing his current status and assets to hard work, respect and God's blessings.
