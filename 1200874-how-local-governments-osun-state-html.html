The role of local government

Osun State - is an inland state that is situated in the south-western part of the country. The current governor of the state is Rauf Aregbesola.

As for local governments, there are 774 LGAs in Nigeria. Each area is subdivided into wards. These are some of the functions of local governments:

  • Local government give economic recommendations to the State;
  • The local government collects fees and taxes;
  • Local government provides maintenance homes for the destitute or infirm people, and other social projects;

Photo: 9jatravel.com.ng

  • Local government gives the licenses for trucks, wheelbarrows, canoes, and bicycles;
  • Local government is responsible for maintenance, regulation and establishment of public conveniences, motor parks and market;
  • Local government is responsible for maintenance and construction of roads, drains, streets, parks, and public highways;
  • Local government handles naming of streets, roads, and numbering of houses;
  • Local government is responsible for maintenance and provision of public transportation and refuse disposal;
  • Local government handles registration of births, deaths, and marriages.
