Olamide biography: age, albums, net worth, house, latest songs

Olamide is a Nigerian hip-hop musician who has attracted a huge following on his social media platforms across Africa. He is known for recording mostly in his native language, Yoruba.

Like any other popular musician, Olamide has released several songs that are enjoyed by his fans. He has worked hard to achieve everything that he has.

Olamide biography

Olamide Adedeji was born on 15th March, 1989 in Bariga, Lagos State. He is the second born child of Pa Adedeji and his second wife, Ronke Osisanya Adedeji. The musician has two siblings, Temmy Gold and Eniola Olamilekan.

Education

He attended his primary and secondary education in Lagos. He, later on, pursued a degree in Mass Communication at the Tai Solarin University of Education.

Career

Olamide's career debut in the music industry was in 2010 when he released his first single, Eni Duro. The song was highly praised by critics and made him instantly famous. The same year, he got to perform at the Hip-Hop World Awards ceremony.

In 2011, he released his first studio album, Rapsodi. The songs from this album were widely played on the best radio stations in Nigeria.

Multiple famous musicians, including D’Banj, Wizkid, ID Cabasa, Reminisce, and 9ice, expressed their wish to collaborate with the young and promising Adedeji.

Adedeji's music does well to bring out a profound cultural aspect of Nigerian culture to anyone that listens to it.

Personal life

The popular musician is a family man who has a wife and three children. His firstborn daughter, Asaiah Monifé Maria, was born to him and Maria Okanrende, a popular on-air personality. Even though the two have a daughter together, their relationship did not last long.

The musician is currently married to Aisha Suleiman. The couple has been together since 2014 and is blessed with two children, namely, Batifeori Maximilliano Adedeji, born on 30th January 2016, and Tunrepin Myles Adebeji, born on 1st July 2019.
