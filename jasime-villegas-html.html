Jasmine Villegas - Age, Family, Bio

R&B singer known as Jasmine V who was signed to Sony Music at the age of sixteen. She signed with Interscope Records in 2013 and released her debut EP, That's Me Right There, in 2014. 

Before Fame

She acted in a commercial for the children's animated film The Land Before Time. She appeared on My Wife and Kids in 2003. 

Trivia

She has collaborated with pop icons like Nick Jonas and Justin Bieber. She appeared in the music video for Bieber's "Baby." 

Family Life

She has two brothers and one step-brother. In February 2016, she welcomed a daughter named Ameera Reign with her boyfriend Ronnie Banks. She and Ronnie got engaged in December 2016, but later called things off. She and Omar Amir welcomed a son named Zayne in May 2020.

Associated With

She had a cameo appearance in Kanye West's "Jesus Walks" music video.
