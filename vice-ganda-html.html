Vice Ganda - Age, Family, Bio

Filipino stand-up comedian who stars in the ABS-CBN variety show It's Showtime. He has starred in two of the highest grossing films in Philippine cinema history.

Before Fame

He studied Political Science at Far Eastern University before beginning to perform stand-up comedy in Manila at age 19.

Trivia

He is known for starring film roles in Praybeyt Benjamin and This Guy's in Love with U Mare!

Family Life

He grew up with four older siblings in Tondo, Manila. His father, a barangay captain, was murdered when he was young. He is openly gay. He has been in a relationship with model Ion Perez.

Associated With

He co-starred in the 2012 comedy This Guy's In Love With U Mare! with Luis Manzano.
