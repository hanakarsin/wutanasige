Ellen McLain - Age, Family, Bio

Voice actress who rose to fame voicing GLaDOS, a character from the Portal video game series. She has also voiced characters in Half-Life 2, Team Fortress 2 and Pacific Rim. She won the Spike Video Game award for Best Performance by a Human Female in 2011. She also teaches voice in children's theater programs. Her ellenmclain Twitter account is mostly used to promote sales of memorabilia and to promote work projects. She has accumulated 20,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She studied at The North Carolina School of the Arts and The New England Conservatory in Boston, eventually earning a master's degree. 

Trivia

She is also an opera singer who sang "Still Alive" and "Want You Gone" for the Portal series. 

Family Life

She married fellow voice actor John Patrick Lowr in 1986. 

Associated With

She acted for Portal 2 alongside Stephen Merchant.
