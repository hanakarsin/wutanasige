Dencia says she does not support marching for abortion

Popular musician and beauty entrepreneur Dencia has never been one to shy away from controversy. However, some feel she crossed the line with her views on feminism.

According to Wikipedia, Feminism is a range of political and social movements that share a common goal: to define, establish, and achieve political, economic, personal, and social rights for women. This includes seeking to establish equal opportunities for women in education and employment.

READ ALSO: Dencia speaks out in support of skin bleaching

But Dencia’s views on the above differ slightly and she let the whole world know this. Speaking via Twitter about the Women’s March that happened all across America over the weekend, she started off by saying despite all the protests, Donald Trump remains President of the whole of America.

From that, she added that Americans are dramatic and women are fighting for what they already have. Instead, they should spend their time fighting for real things like HIV stigmatization and not rights for abortion.
