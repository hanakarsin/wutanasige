Valentine Ozigbo Emerges PDP Flag Bearer

published on February 27, 2024
A former managing director of Transcorp Plc, Mr. Valentine Ozigbo has emerged as the official Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) candidate for the November 6 Anambra State governorship election. He emerged after winning the primary election held at the Dora Akunyili Women Development Center in Awka, Anambra State on Saturday. Naija News understands Mr. Ozigbo polled 62 votes to defeat other aspirants. Other aspirants at the primary election and their votes were Obiora Okonkwo -58, Senator Uche Ekwunife -44, Winston Use – 12, Chris Azubogu -10, Godwin Maduka -5 and Genevieve Ekwochi -3, Walter Okeke – 2 and Chidi Onyemelukwe – 2.
