Chelsea Agree Deal With Monaco For Axel Disasi

published on June 16, 2024
Chelsea have agreed a deal with French Ligue 1 club, Monaco for the services of French center-back Axel Disasi worth about £38.6 million. Axel Disasi, 25, has just two years left on his contract with the French Ligue 1 side. Due to his prolific performance last season, a series of Premier League clubs including Manchester United, Newcastle United, and Chelsea showed interest in him. At the end of the dragging transfer negotiations, Monaco accepted Chelsea’s offer after the French side had reached an agreement with Championship side, Southampton, for Ghanian defender Mohammed Salisu as Disasi’s replacement.
