Osinbajo Go Spiritual, Leads Christian Clergies To Pray For Nigeria

published on May 30, 2024
Nigeria Vice President Yemi Osinbajo has gone spiritual over the deteriorating security situations in the country. Naija News reports that Osinbajo on Sunday led a group of pastors in praying to God for an end to insurgent attacks and all forms of bloodshed in the country. Osinbajo was a special guest where two churches were commissioned in Abuja on Sunday. Addressing the Christian worshippers at the commissioning of the churches; All Saints Anglican Church Wuse Zone 5, and the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) City of David Parish, Utako, the vice president said the occasion must be seen as a call to the greater work of transforming the entire society.
