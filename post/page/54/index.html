NFF Appoints New Coach For Falcons

published on July 15, 2024
Former Trinidad and Tobago coach, Randy Waldrum has been appointed by the Nigerian Football Federation as the new head coach of the Super Falcons. Waldrum’s appointment was announced in a statement made available to newsmen by the Communications Department of the NFF. Recall that the former coach, Thomas Dennerby, resigned from the Falcons job, a year ago. According to the NFF, consulting firm, Phillips Consulting, helped in Waldrum’s appointment.
