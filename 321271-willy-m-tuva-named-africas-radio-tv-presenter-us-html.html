Willy M Tuva named Africa's best radio, TV presenter in US

- Tuva was recognised as Africa's best radio and TV presenter during AFRIMMA 2019

- Tanzania musician Ommy Dimpoz won East African male artiste of the year

- Award for the best DJ from East Africa was won by DJ Poison Ivy

Radio presenter Mzazi Willy M Tuva has merged as Africa's best radio and TV presenter during the Muzik Magazine Awards (AFRIMMA) competitions 2019.

The media personality best known for his famous Mambo Mseto show which airs on both Citizen Radio and Citizen TV beat other competitors to win the prestigious award during an event that was held in Dallas, Texas, USA, on Saturday, October 26.

Tanzanian Ommy Dimpoz floored a crowded field of East African artistes including Diamond, Ali Kiba, Nyanshinski, Kaligraph Jones, Ravvany among others to win East African male artiste of the year.

Award for the best DJ from East Africa was won by DJ Poison Ivy, the first woman ever to DJ an NBA All-Star game, and the first Kenyan female DJ of an NBA franchise.

Toofan floored Sauti Sol which was nominated for the best African group.

Kenya was represented across the various categories by Sauti Sol, Nyashinski, Kaligraph Jones, Poison ivy and the man of the night Mzazi Willy M. Tuva.

In 2012, he also won the AFRIMA award as the best radio presenter in East Africa.

The Mambo Mseto East Africa show has been ruling the airwaves for a long time before it was extended to air on TV as from 4pm Monday to Friday.

The down to earth TV host also runs a foundation that has helped so many helpless Kenyans especially those on the streets.
