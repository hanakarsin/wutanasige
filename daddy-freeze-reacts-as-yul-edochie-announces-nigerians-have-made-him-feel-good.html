Daddy Freeze Reacts As Yul Edochie Announces Nigerians Have Made Him 'Feel Good'

Media personality Daddy Freeze has reacted to Yul Edochie statement of Nigerians making him feel good for breaking the internet

Recall that Yul had taken to his Instagram account to announce the birth of his son and marriage to his colleague Judy Austin.

Nigerians, especially women expressed disappointment at Yul’s decision to take another wife considering that the first one had being with him for 17 years.

However, Yul remains unfazed despite the heat from people. Instead he has praised himself for the decision he made and has once again stated via his Instagram account that he has being the most talked about man in the planet for two weeks and this makes him feel good.

Reacting to the post, Freeze condemned Yul’s lack of remorse for his actions, saying that the actor lacked emotional intelligence.

He wrote: Where’s his emotional intelligence.

Meanwhile, controversial Nollywood actor and producer, Uche Madwagwu has said that the reason his senior colleague, Genevive Nnaji is often not on social media is because of her man.

Madwagwu took to his Instagram account to question the veteran feminism for instantly agreeing to her man’s directive to allegedly stay off social media.
