3 Times Femi Otedola Has Shared Father-Son Moment with Fewa as the Young Man Clocks 21

Billionaire businessman Femi Otedola's son Fewa clocked a new age on Wednesday, June 2, and friends and family have showered encomium on the young man who happens to be the only male child of the oil magnate.

Legit.ng presents three interesting moments between Femi and his son Fewa.

1. Daddy celebrates his son's birthday

Femi Otedola took to Instagram to celebrate Fewa as he clocked 21. In the adorable photo Femi posted on his page, he could be seen showing Fewa something on his phone as father and son wore beautiful smiles.

The businessman wrote:

"Happy Birthday to my Fewa Boy!! Papa loves you so much."

2. Father and son cooking in the kitchen

If there is one thing people love Femi for, it is the fact that he creates time for his family to spend beautiful moments with them.
