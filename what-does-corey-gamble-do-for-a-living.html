Kris Jenner's Partner Corey Gamble Is Living His Best Life

Fans of the Kardashians have watched Kris Jenner date Corey Gamble for years now. But what does he do for a living? Details inside.

Fans of the Kardashians (and their many ventures) know that keeping up with the family is basically a full-time job. Between all of the friendships, feuds, relationships, marriages, divorces, and births going on in the family, there's a lot to keep up with when it comes to the Kardashian extended universe.

Corey Gamble has been dating Kardashian matriarch Kris Jenner since 2014 when they were photographed while taking a romantic Mexican vacation together. Since then, the couple has been going strong — but what does Corey actually do for a living? Here's what we know.

So, what does Corey Gamble do for a living?

According to Corey's Facebook profile, the Atlanta native attended Westlake High School and Morehouse College and studied business marketing as an undergrad. His Linkedin page even mentions that his Bachelor of Business Administration focused on "Business, Management, Marketing, and Related Support Services."

Sometime between graduating and starting his relationship with Kris, Corey reportedly worked as a road manager with controversial music manager Scooter Braun. Corey and Justin have developed such a tight friendship over the years that Biebs often appears on his Instagram page, and Corey refers to the "Baby" singer as his "nephew."

However, as far as occupations are concerned, it appears Corey's is taking care of Kris, chilling in lavish yachts, and wearing Rolexes and fancy suits. He also has a pretty interesting side hustle, though. In 2023, he released a luxury clothing line through Dolce & Gabbana. The line features a number silk garments ranging in price from $995 to $2,695.

What is Corey Gamble's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the TV personality is worth a whopping $2.5 million. 

And before being known for dating the most famous matriarch in Hollywood, Corey was in a relationship with another reality TV star: Sheree Buchanan from VH1's Atlanta Exes, reports the outlet. Sheree was on the show as NFL player Ray Buchanan's ex-wife.

On both Keeping Up With the Kardashians and Kardashians, Corey is known for keeping things a bit more low-key than the rest of the family. As the Los Angeles Times put it, "he doesn't drink or party." "He's very caring about other people and generally just an all-around good guy," they wrote back when his relationship with Kris was just a rumor.

But that doesn't mean he won't clap back to his haters on social media. In September of 2019, Corey posted a video on Instagram of himself sipping wine on a yacht wearing an expensive watch and a Rolex shirt. "What is your job, man?" wrote one of his followers.

Corey replied, "Started out washing cars, cutting grass... & bagging groceries at Kroger... what do u do today ????"

Touché, Corey. Touché.
