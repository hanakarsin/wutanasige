Lil Tay - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper and Instagram phenomenon who gained fame in 2018 by posting pictures and videos in which she was flaunting money and luxury cars. She has earned over 5 million Instagram followers.  She released the song "Sucker 4 Green" in September of 2023 and the music video quickly was trending on YouTube.

Before Fame

She took singing and dancing lessons growing up. 

Trivia

She was interviewed alongside her mother by ABC's Juju Chang in May 2018. Her first Instagram account was deleted in the summer of 2018. She made a return to Instagram to honor the late Xxxtentacion after his death, making his picture her profile icon.

Family Life

She has lived in Vancouver. She has a brother named Jason and her parents' names are Angela Tian and Christopher J. Hope. Her mother is a real estate agent. Her brother is named Jason Tian.

Associated With

She became friends with fellow web star Woah Vicky. They appeared on each other's social feeds. She also posted a picture with Lil Pump, which received over 500,000 likes on Instagram.
