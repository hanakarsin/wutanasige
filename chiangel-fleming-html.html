Young Reborn Mommy - Age, Family, Bio

Vlogger named Chiangel Fleming whose YouTube channel, Young Reborn Mommy, revolves around her life as a "mother" of a lifelike reborn baby doll. She creates her own reborn dolls through her own company, Chi'Angel Loves Reborns Creations. She welcomed a real child into the world. Her YouTube videos have been viewed more than 37 million times.

Before Fame

She grew up in Franklin, Louisiana, where she attended Franklin Jr. High. 

Trivia

Her most popular video, "Reborn Baby Goes Shopping" has over 7.6 million views.

Family Life

She has sisters. She welcomed her first child in March 2021. 

Associated With

Like JuliesReborns, she is famous for her reborn doll channel.
