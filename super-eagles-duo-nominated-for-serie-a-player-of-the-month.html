Super Eagles Duo Nominated For Serie A Player Of The Month

Super Eagles duo, Victor Osimhen and Ademola Lookman has been named among nominees for the Serie A Player of the Month for October

The two Nigerian International put up outstanding performances for their respective Italian clubs by scoring crucial goals.

In five games for Atalanta in October, former Leicester forward, Lookman, scored four goals against Empoli, Sassuolo, Fiorentina, and Udinese.

Naija News earlier reported that the Nigerian international stated that he would not stop giving such an impressive performance.

Meanwhile, Osimhen has been in an excellent shape following his recovery from the injury he picked up in September.

In three league games during the month of October, the 23-year-old scored five goals.
