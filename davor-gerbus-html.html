Davor Gerbus - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube vlogger known for the joint channel thelazyWAVE shared with his good friend Petra. Together they post vlogs, tags, and challenges.  

Before Fame

He first made his entry into the social media world with Instagram in the summer of 2012, two years before the creation of his YouTube channel.

Trivia

Some of thelazyWAVE's most popular videos include "7 SECONDS CHALLENGE" and "Who twerked better?" At the end of 2021, he took a trip to Sri Lanka and posted clips of his time there to his popular gerbusdavor TikTok channel. He has seen his channel build a following of over 40,000 fans. His YouTube channel also features vlogs from his international travels.  

Family Life

He is from Zagreb, Croatia. He is currently dating the social media influencer Vanja Veljic.

Associated With

He posted photos and videos from a Nicki Minaj concert in March 2015 to his Instagram account.
