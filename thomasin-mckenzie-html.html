Thomasin McKenzie - Age, Family, Bio

Movie actress who signed on to appear in the films The King and Jojo Rabbit. 

Before Fame

Her first on-screen role was as The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies in 2014.

Trivia

She played the title role in the 2017 TV show Lucy Lewis Can't Lose. She acted in the 2019 Netflix movie The King. 

Family Life

Her mother is Miranda Harcourt.

Associated With

She acted alongside Martin Freeman in The Hobbit.
