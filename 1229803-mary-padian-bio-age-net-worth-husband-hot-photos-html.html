Who is Mary Padian?

Mary Padian is a well-known television personality and businesswoman. She gained fame through her participation in the reality TV series Storage Wars.

Mary was born to Teresa (Lebanese) and John Padian (Irish). She has a brother named Luke Padian. The TV star earned a bachelor's degree in Photojournalism from the University of Texas in 2003.

How old is Mary Padian?

Mary was born on 24 August 1980 in Dallas, Texas, USA. She is 43 years old as of 2024. Her zodiac sign is Virgo.

Career

Mary was an intern at D Magazine after graduating. She later landed the position of assistant editor for Architectural Digest (an international design magazine) in New York.

Mary rekindled her interest in collecting and repurposing old items and made videos about it under the name Mary's Finds. In 2010, she opened a store in Dallas, Texas, under the same name.

Mary Padian's Storage Wars career

Mary's customer, Morris Moe Prigoff, helped her join Storage Wars in 2012. Moe was one of the reality show's cast members and was moved by what Mary Padian was doing in her store.

Mary was referred to as "The Junker" on the show because she is not afraid of getting down and dirty when looking for items to fix. As a result, she became popular and was cast in the original Storage Wars fifth season. Mary has made on-and-off appearances in the subsequent seasons.
