Whenever I Or Femi Adesina Speak On Behalf Of Buhari, The President Has Addressed Nigerians

published on April 21, 2024
The Presidency on Monday submitted that it is not compulsory for President Muhammadu Buhari to personally speak on every matter. The Senior Special Assistant to the President on Media and Publicity, Garba Shehu who made this position known on Monday, said Buhari believes in action more than words. He added that whenever he or the special adviser to the President on media and publicity, Femi Adesina speaks on behalf of the President, then Buhari has spoken to Nigerians on the issue and citizens should learn to accept that fact.
