Bandits Invade Community In Zamfara, Abduct Newly Married Couple

published on August 13, 2024
A newly married couple was abducted at the Madabanciya community in Bungudu local government area of Zamfara State on Sunday night. Naija News learnt that the gunmen reportedly stormed the village on foot around 11.45pm on Sunday, leaving their motorcycles outside the town. An indigene of the area, Ibrahim Sani, who spoke with PUNCH on the incident, said the bandits, who were in large numbers, were wielding sophisticated weapons and began shooting indiscriminately to scare residents when they arrived.
