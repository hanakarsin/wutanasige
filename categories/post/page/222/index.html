South Call For Presidency Democracy But Rotational Presidency Unconstitutional

published on April 04, 2024
Governor Yahaya Bello of Kogi State has described calls by the southern governors forum for President Muhammadu Buhari’s successor to come from their region as democracy. He stated this while speaking at the inauguration of a presidential campaign movement – Movement called Yahaya Bello Network Group (YBN) – for him by some northern youth in Kaduna. However, Bello, who was represented at the event by his Special Adviser on Youth and Student Affairs, Ahmed Jubril, said rotational presidency is not in the constitution of the ruling All Progressives Congress (APC).
