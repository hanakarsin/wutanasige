Lagos Lawmakers Approves Sanwo-Olu's Request For N85bn Bond

published on February 25, 2024
Governor Babajide Sanwo-Olu’s request of ₦85 billion special dispensation for bond redemption as well as ₦85 billion for bridge loan for a quarter has been approved by the Lagos State Assembly. This is in pursuant to the approval of Bonds, Loans and other Security Law of Lagos State 2015 and comes at 9.25% per annum to bond financing programme. The Chairman of the Committee on Finance, Hon. Rotimi Olowo, at a sitting on Monday stated that the terms of reference of the request received from the executive arm appraise the request for a special dispensation; enquire into the debt profile of the State and ascertain the source of the fund for repayment and refinancing.
